TAMID Keyrus Data Science Group

"To give college students the skills and the opportunity to make a difference with data science on a worldwide scale"
Overview

TAMID-Keyrus Data Science Group offers students with opportunity to learn about data science skills, applying those skills to gain professional, enterprise, and real-world experience with data science, while simultaneously serving the public (and assisting private enterprises) to shine light on the underlying truth and make the best decisions.

TAMID-Keyrus Data Science Group revolves around two critical pillars–education curriculum and ventures. Education curriculum is comprised of 4 to 6 weeks of training on the fundamentals of data science, with weekly meetings, where all members learn, explore, and apply a novel data science technique or a model. Ventures, i.e. case projects, are data science based case projects, e.g. application of data science models to a business model, for startups, nonprofits, or private enterprises. Ventures will offer professional data science experience. This standardized structure of TAMID–Keyrus Data Science Group allow for massive scalability for all college campuses.

History

TAMID-Keyrus Data Science Group's idea was first conceived sometime during the summer of 2017.
